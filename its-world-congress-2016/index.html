About SoftwareMill

SoftwareMill, founded in 2009, develops custom software solutions for a variety of industries: transportation, logistics, communication, banking, energy and entertainment.

 

This allowed us to gain a lot of experience in developing a wide range of systems, including various big-data and fast-data projects, data analytics and machine learning. We specialize in high-performance, distributed backend systems, as well as web and mobile apps.

 

Our company is a Select Consulting Partner of Lightbend, Confluent, as well as Datastax. SoftwareMill, Lightbend, Confluent and Datastax cooperate to deliver top-notch consultancy and development services in various areas, including big data development.
